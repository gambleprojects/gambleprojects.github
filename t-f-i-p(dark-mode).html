As the viedo has stated programing is important, because it allows us to be creative, help others, and further connect the world. Programing is also important, because it can be used in almost every industry such as: the secerity industry, the oil industry, the farming industry, etc. By learning programing you have the ability to get in to almost every industry, the ability to help people, and the ability to help further connect the world.

Programing is the future, because no mater how far we go into the future there will always be a need for computer programers and developers (unless some catastrofic event happens). The Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) predicts that the job outlook for software developers will increse by 24% between 2016 and 2026 which is much fast than average growth. It is one of the fastest growing jobs in America.
