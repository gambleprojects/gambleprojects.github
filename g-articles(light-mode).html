Pablo Picasso

By: Camden B. Gamble

#History

Pablo Picasso is one of the most renowned artists of all time. His unique artistic ability attracted people from all over the globe and it is still having effects on people today. Pablo Picasso was born in Spain in the year of 1881, years later he moved to France, which is where he would live for most of his adult life. He lived until 1973, that made him 91 years old in the year of his death.
